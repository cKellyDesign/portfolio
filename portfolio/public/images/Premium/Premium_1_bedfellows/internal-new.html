Furniture

Bedfellows is your premier destination for luxurious, custom-designed furniture for the entire home. We offer a vast and eclectic array of contemporary and vintage styles to adorn and distinguish your living and sleeping spaces.

We offer superb, hand-crafted bedroom furniture from the likes of Copeland of Vermont, and name-brand mattresses of natural latex and all-cotton innerspring from Aireloom and Royal-Pedic.

Dream you're adrift in heaven and wake up feeling rejuvenated with extravagant, specialty mattresses from the likes of Somma Flotation, Tempur-Pedic memory foam and Comfortaire air support. We also carry sensuous, 100% cotton European bedding from Schlossberg of Switzerland.

Bedfellows isn't only about the bedroom though! We offer elegant contemporary sofas and sofa beds from Dellarobbia and American Leather, as well as unique lamps and custom lighting, mid-century vintage furniture and art and much, much more.
