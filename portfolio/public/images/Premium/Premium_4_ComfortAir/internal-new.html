About Us

Blending Expertise and Experience

Since 1946, valley professionals have looked to Comfort Air to provide expert mechanical "Design & Build" services. We provide a full range of heating and air conditioning innovations, specializing in sophisticated equipment, environmental control and direct digital control systems for commercial, medical and hi-tech facilities.

Owners, architects and general contractors all turn to Comfort Air for the level of professionalism we provide. Our engineering staff will work with both the architect and the building owner to determine their needs and to establish the best solutions for any given project.

We then provide accurate estimates which truly reflect anticipated costs, and we always work to keep change orders to a minimum. Once a project is underway, the Comfort Air team coordinates scheduling with other trades and affiliated professionals to smooth the path to completion. To ensure all systems remain at peak performance, maintenance and service are available 24 hours every day from our well-staffed service department.

With the focus on energy conservation, Comfort Air stays abreast of the latest technological developments which can save energy and lower the lifecycle costs of a facility. Our company brings that expertise to every new project, helping owners and architects take advantage of equipment that balances energy savings with comfort.

Project types include:

  • Hospitals, including Surgical Suites
  • Single and Multi-Story Office Buildings
  • High-tech Facilities, including Clean Rooms
  • Educational Facilities
  • Institutional Facilities
  • Laboratories
  • Manufacturing Facilities
  • Warehouses
  • And More…
